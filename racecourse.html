Reading the Course

Start: cross the line between the committee boat and the pin, sailing upwind. Windward mark: round to port, then the short hop to the offset mark keeps boats clear of traffic still beating up. Leeward gate: two orange tetrahedrons — pick either one, round it the natural way (left mark to port, right mark to starboard), and head back upwind. Picking the gate side that takes you toward the favored side of the next beat is free distance.

Triangle (Olympic): beat to the windward mark, bear away onto the reach to the jibe mark, jibe, reach back down to the leeward mark, and beat again. Reaches reward clear air — defend high early, then drive low for speed.
